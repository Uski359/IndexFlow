# Execution Agreement (Wyoming LLC Variant)

> **Draft template. Obtain U.S. counsel sign-off before use.**

## Parties
- **Company:** IndexFlow Labs LLC (Wyoming) acting via its managers.
- **DAO-lite:** Governance committee coordinating proposals and dry-run votes.

## Purpose
- Delegate day-to-day execution authority to the Company while DAO-lite frames strategic intent.
- Maintain a paper trail linking DAO-lite proposals to Company-approved expenditures.

## Key Clauses
- **Authorization:** DAO-lite recommendations require written manager approval before funds move.
- **Compliance:** Company ensures OFAC/KYC policies, tax filings, and insurance coverage.
- **Reporting:** DAO-lite delivers monthly governance summaries; Company provides financial statements quarterly.

## Suspension
- Managers may pause DAO-lite execution authority immediately if compliance risks emerge.
- DAO-lite may request review of any manager decision within 3 business days.

## Liability
- DAO-lite members operate as advisors; fiduciary duties rest with the Company.
- No digital asset custody is delegated under this agreement.
